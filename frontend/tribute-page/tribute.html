About him:

Joaquim Benedito Barbosa Gomes, born in Paracatu, MG, Brazil is a former Justice of the Supreme Federal Court in Brazil. He served as the president of the court (Chief Justice) between 2012 and 2014. Barbosa studied law at University of Brasília (1979) and holds a master's degree (1990) and a doctorate (1993) from Panthéon-Assas University. In 2013, he was elected by Time magazine as one of the 100 most influential people in the world.
